What types of rewards should I offer?

You can offer any Drop In Reward you wish to encourage users to visit your bar. We recommend offering enticing rewards to Drop In users, which may include the following:

  • Discounted drink & food coupons
  • % or $’s off total bill (i.e., $5 off when you spend $25 or more)
  • Free cover for entry or “line cuts”
  • Personal happy hour deals
  • Other drink & food specials

Remember, the cost of this reward represents the cost for encouraging a customer to visit your bar over others in the community! Selecting an enticing reward can not only help you acquire new customers, but retain loyal customers as well.

Want to change your Drop In Reward? Simply contact our team at any time at bars@joindropin.com.

Is a user's loyalty level specific to each bar?

No. Credits earned at any participating bar will contribute to your Drop In loyalty level. This loyalty level will be applicable at all participating bars in the Drop In community. Additionally, when rewards are received, they are eligible for redemption at any bar in the Drop In community.

How do I update my bar's information on Drop In?

You can update your bar's information (including "about us", pictures, contact info & current specials) simply by contacting our team at bars@joindropin.com.

I noticed I am missing an iBeacon in my bar, what should I do?

If one of you iBeacons falls, is lost or is not working properly, please immediately contact our team at bars@joindropin.com and we will disable and replace it as soon as possible.

How do I know a user is redeeming a valid Drop In Reward?

Simply look for the reward redemption (or countdown) screen and confirm it is decreasing. This ensures the reward is active and valid. A customer will have 120 seconds to show you the screen once they hit the redeem button.
